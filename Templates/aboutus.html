To crack group1 exams follow our 7 Step Plan

We are providing courses for SSC, BANK, GROUP1, GROUP2

Group1 exams in India are highly competitive and challenging. These exams are conducted by various state governments and are aimed at selecting candidates for top-level administrative positions in the government. To crack the group1 exams in India, candidates need to have a thorough understanding of the Indian political, social, and economic system. They also need to have a good grasp of current affairs and general knowledge.

Here are some tips from Neomentor Experts that can help you prepare for group1 exams in India:

  • Understand the exam pattern: It's important to know the exam pattern before you start preparing. The group1 exams usually consist of two or three stages, including a preliminary exam, a main exam, and an interview. Understanding the exam pattern can help you plan your preparation accordingly.

  • Prepare a study plan: Once you know the exam pattern, create a study plan. Set daily, weekly, and monthly goals for yourself and stick to them. Make sure to allocate enough time for each subject and topic.

  • Focus on the basics: Make sure to have a strong foundation in the basics of all the subjects. This will help you tackle the advanced topics with ease.

  • Read newspapers and magazines: Reading newspapers and magazines is a great way to stay updated on current affairs. Make sure to read a newspaper every day and take notes on important events and news.

  • previous year papers: Solve as many previous year papers as you can. This will give you an idea of the type of questions that are asked in the exam and help you understand the exam pattern better.

  • Take mock tests: Taking mock tests is a great way to evaluate your preparation. It will help you identify your strengths and weaknesses and work on them accordingly.

  • Stay positive and motivated: Stay positive and motivated throughout your preparation. Group1 exams are tough, and it's normal to feel overwhelmed at times. But remember to stay focused and keep working hard.

  • In conclusion, preparing for group1 exams in India requires a lot of hard work and dedication. Make sure to follow a structured study plan provided by Neomentors, focus on the basics, stay updated on current affairs, and practice regularly. With the right approach and mindset, you can crack the exam and achieve your goal of becoming a top-level administrator in the government. Good luck!
